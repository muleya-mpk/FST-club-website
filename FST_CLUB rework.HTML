Who We Are

Bridging Science &
Industry

The BUAN Food Science and Technology Club is a student-led organisation at the Botswana University of Agriculture and Natural Resources, located at Content Farm, Sebele, Gaborone. We bring together students, faculty, and industry professionals who share a passion for food science and technology.

Our club provides a platform for members to develop professionally and academically — through field trips, speaker sessions, demonstrations, and industry networking — all while building civic and social responsibility within the food sector.

Science-Driven

Grounded in rigorous food science principles and research.

Industry-Linked

Direct connections to food industry professionals and companies.

Community

An inclusive and welcoming environment for all food science enthusiasts.

Innovation

Encouraging creativity and forward thinking in food technology.

Our Purpose

Club
Objectives

The FST Club is guided by a clear set of objectives drawn from our constitution. These principles shape every event, program, and initiative we undertake to advance food science at BUAN and beyond.

Foster Relationships

Build meaningful connections among students and faculty in the Food Science and Technology Department.

Scope of FST

Acquaint members with the full breadth and depth of Food Science and Technology as a discipline.

Corporate Affiliation

Affiliate with corporate and other bodies that assist in advocating for the interests of the club.

Business Development

Organise services that improve member performance and their meaningful role in Botswana's economy.

Communication & Linkages

Promote cooperation and networking among members and food industry stakeholders.

Community Cooperation

Work with local authorities and bodies to advance the interests of members and the wider community.

Membership
Classes

Membership is open to all students interested in Food Science and Technology. No applicant may be denied membership on the basis of race, colour, gender, sexual orientation, age, religion, or national origin.

Members are expected to uphold club regulations, wear club uniforms at events, and actively support the club's objectives and activities.
